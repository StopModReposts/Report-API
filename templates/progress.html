This page visualizes the current progress of reviewing the reported sites (False-Positives included).

Why does the progress go down sometimes?

We add sites to our list in badges. The current progress is the progress for the next batch. When we're ready, the sites marked as reviewed will be added to / removed from our list and the progress will return back to zero.

Why is it taking so long?

We're a group of volunteers, so we don't always have time to review sites. We also want to reduce the amount of False-Postives, so site reviews can take a bit.
